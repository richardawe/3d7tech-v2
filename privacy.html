Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

3D7 Tech ("we", "us") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

Who we are

3D7 Tech is a Business Transformation & AI Automation consultancy based in North West England. Contact us at richard3d7@gmail.com.

What data we collect

  • Contact information — name, work email, and company name when you download our guide or submit an enquiry.
  • Usage data — pages visited, scroll depth, and clicks on this site, if you accept analytics cookies.
  • Outreach context — if you arrive via a personalised link, we may have sourced your publicly available business information (company name, sector, website) from Companies House or similar public sources as part of our legitimate interest in promoting our services to relevant businesses.

Why we collect it

  • To deliver what you requested — sending you the guide or responding to an enquiry.
  • Legitimate interest (B2B outreach) — contacting businesses that may benefit from our services, using publicly available information. You can opt out at any time.
  • Analytics — understanding how our site is used so we can improve it (only with your consent).

How we use your data

We use your contact information to send you the content you requested and occasional relevant tips on process improvement and AI for SMEs. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties except our email delivery provider (Resend) and our form processor (Formspree), both of which are GDPR-compliant processors.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request deletion, object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at richard3d7@gmail.com.

Cookies

We only use analytics cookies, and only with your explicit consent via the banner shown on your first visit. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. You can decline at any time — this does not affect site functionality.

Unsubscribe / opt out

To be removed from all 3D7 Tech communications immediately and permanently, email us directly. We will process your request within 24 hours and you will not be contacted again.
